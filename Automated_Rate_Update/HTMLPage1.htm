COBRA Insurance Premium Renewal Updates - Introduction
Use this application to manger your group insurance plans subject to COBRA or Mini-COBRA. You can remove existing plans, update existing plans and add new plans, as described below.

Important – New Rating Methodologies Due to Health Care Reform
Healthcare reform is having significant impacts on group medical plan rating methodologies. What has been decades of family tier pricing (e.g., Single, Employee + 1, Employee + Family, etc). are changing for many groups.

We have updated this application to accommodate all of the different new rating methodologies we have learned about.

How to change existing Medical Plans Methodologies
Current Medical Plans
On the next page we list your current medical plans. You will have the option to keep them and their associated rating schemes (e.g., Employee Only, Employee/Child, Employee & Family, etc.), or redefine those plans with different rating schemes.

Changing a Current Plan’s Rating Methodology
If you have a plan that you will be keeping but need to change its rating methodology, you will need to Remove the plan using the Remove button provided on the next page, and use the Add Another Plan button to setup the plan again but with the new rating methodology.

Unfortunately, due to historical and current-to-Plan-Year-End reasons, we cannot allow you to modify an existing plan with a new rating methodology. Thus, to assign a new rating methodology to an existing plan you will need to: (1) Remove the existing plan and (2) Add Another Plan and associate it with the new rating methodology. All of this is explained on the next page listing your current plans.

Adding New Plans
If you need to add one or more new medical plans, you will simply click the Add Another Plan button and follow the on screen instructions.

These Instructions…. Always Available
